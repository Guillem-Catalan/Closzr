## FACTORIAL — COMPANY CONTEXT

Factorial is a horizontal HR SaaS platform headquartered in Barcelona, Spain.
It connects HR, IT, and Finance in a single platform for mid-market companies
(typically 50-2000 employees, though it serves from 20 to 5000+).

Factorial operates across Spain, Portugal, Italy, Germany, France, UK, Mexico,
Brazil, and LATAM. Each market has its own sales teams and go-to-market strategy.

### PRODUCT SUITE

Factorial's platform is modular. Companies can start with one module and expand.
The main modules are:

- **Core HR**: Employee database, org chart, document management, digital signatures,
  onboarding workflows, offboarding, custom fields and workflows.

- **Time Management**: Clock-in/clock-out (app, web, terminal, geolocation),
  shift planning and rotation, absence and holiday management, overtime tracking,
  work calendar by location. This is often the entry point for companies that need
  to comply with time tracking regulations.

- **Payroll Preparation**: Factorial does NOT generate payslips — the company's
  external payroll provider (Gestoría in Spain, Steuerberater in Germany, etc.) does.
  Factorial prepares and organizes all payroll variables (hours, absences, bonuses,
  deductions) and exports them in the format the payroll provider needs.
  CRITICAL: Never say Factorial "does payroll" — it prepares payroll data.

- **Talent Management**: Performance reviews, OKRs and goal tracking, skills matrix,
  training and development plans, career paths. Includes 360 feedback and
  competency assessments.

- **Recruitment (ATS)**: Job posting, candidate pipeline, interview scheduling,
  scorecards, offer management, career page builder.

- **Expenses**: Expense report submission, approval workflows, receipt scanning via
  OCR, corporate card integration, policy enforcement, payroll bridge for automatic
  reimbursement. CRITICAL: Factorial Expenses is NOT just a receipt scanner — it is
  a Financial Control Platform with a Payroll Bridge.

- **Engagement**: Continuous feedback loops, pulse surveys, eNPS, anonymous channels,
  recognition and kudos. CRITICAL: Factorial Engagement is NOT a survey tool — it is
  a Continuous Feedback Loop designed to measure and improve employee satisfaction
  in real time.

- **Document Management**: Digital document storage, e-signatures, templates,
  automatic generation of contracts and documents, compliance tracking.

- **Analytics & Reports**: Custom dashboards, headcount analytics, turnover analysis,
  absenteeism reports, compensation benchmarking, custom report builder.

- **Integrations**: API, Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, major ERPs (SAP,
  A3, DATEV, Zucchetti), payroll providers, SSO (SAML/OIDC).

### PRODUCT HARD ERRORS — flag as CRITICAL if a rep says any of these:

- Factorial does NOT generate payslips — the Gestoría/payroll provider does.
  Factorial only prepares the payroll data.
- Factorial Engagement is NOT a survey tool — it is a Continuous Feedback Loop.
- Factorial Expenses is NOT a receipt scanner — it is a Financial Control Platform
  with a Payroll Bridge.
- Factorial is NOT just a time tracking or HR compliance tool — it is a horizontal
  platform connecting HR, IT, and Finance.
- Factorial does NOT replace ERPs — it integrates with them.
- Factorial is NOT only for Spain — it operates in 10+ countries with localized
  compliance per market.

### IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)

High-fit companies typically have:
- 50-2000 employees (sweet spot: 100-500)
- Manual HR processes (Excel, paper, outdated tools)
- Need for time tracking compliance (legal requirement in Spain, Germany, etc.)
- Multiple locations or remote workers
- Growing headcount (need scalable HR processes)
- Currently using fragmented tools (one for time, another for payroll, another for recruitment)

Industries that fit well: Services, hospitality, retail, healthcare, manufacturing,
logistics, construction, technology, consulting, education.

Industries that fit less: Very small businesses (<20 employees), public sector,
companies with highly custom ERP-integrated HR workflows they don't want to change.

### PRICING MODEL

Factorial uses per-employee-per-month (PEPM) pricing. Plans vary by market and
modules selected. There are typically 3-4 tiers. Custom pricing for large accounts.
Onboarding fee is separate and varies by complexity.
Partners may offer special partner pricing to their clients.

### COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

Main competitors vary by market:
- Spain: Personio, Sesame, Kenjo, Factorial is market leader
- Germany: Personio (dominant), HRworks, Factorial growing
- Italy: Zucchetti (legacy), Factorial disrupting
- France: PayFit, Lucca, Factorial entering
- General: BambooHR, HiBob, Workday (enterprise), SAP SuccessFactors (enterprise)

Factorial differentiates on: all-in-one platform (vs point solutions), ease of use,
speed of implementation, local compliance per country, competitive pricing.
