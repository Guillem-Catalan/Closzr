## FACTORIAL PRODUCT CATALOG — for product intelligence analysis

Each module solves specific pains. When analyzing conversations, map prospect pains
to these modules. When recommending, connect the product to the pain the prospect
actually expressed — never do a generic feature dump.

Use pricing to estimate MRR impact of recommendations. Architecture: per-seat means
price × employees/month. Fixed means flat fee/month. Business and Enterprise are tiers.
